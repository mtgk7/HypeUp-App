# HypeUp — SMM Panel

## Product Purpose
HypeUp is a social media marketing (SMM) panel targeting Turkish users. Customers buy followers, likes, views and other engagement services for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X, Telegram, Facebook, Spotify, Discord and Pinterest. Orders are fulfilled automatically via PRM4U API.

## Users
- Turkish social media users, influencers, small business owners aged 18–35
- Digital marketing agencies buying in bulk
- Resellers running their own SMM operations

## Register
brand (the landing page IS the product — it drives conversion)

## Tone
Confident, modern, efficient. Not corporate. Not hype-bro. Premium but accessible.

## Anti-references
- Cheap-looking panels with neon colors and spinning text
- Generic SaaS cream with teal accents
- Glassmorphism everywhere
- Obvious crypto-dark aesthetics (neon on pure black)

## Brand color
Violet/purple as primary accent — already established in the codebase (violet-600).

## Key value props
1. Instant delivery
2. Affordable prices in Turkish Lira
3. 9 platforms in one panel
4. 7/24 support
